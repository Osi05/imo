Lead Product Designer
Company
Lenco Technologies
Industry
Financial Services
Lenco launched in 2021 as a digital only business bank supporting business of all sizes and enabling business owners create and manage a business current account without visiting a physical outlet and overcoming the hassles of opening an account
Goal
Redesign of the Lenco landing page to create a user friendly interface that focuses on simplicity and highlighting the core products offered
My Role & Approach

I joined the team as the sole designer and I collaborated with key stake holders, business support and marketing unit to understand the business goals and priorities.

 

I collected some useful data with the help of third party tools like HotJar and Google analytics which were already implemented on the website. We discovered unique patterns of usage and information structure. Our insights helped us to establish a baseline for future improvement and also retain some familiarity with the exisiting interface

Previous Landing Page

We drafted out the layout, navigation design, call to action, updating the content on the landing page and setting the tone of voice in order to improve the messaging so it can be easily understood by all users and doing away with vague and ambiguous terms

 

I lead the redesign working with Figma and collaborated with developers on Trello to build the project. We followed an agile project management framework for this task which helped us in iterating and testing the ideas faster.

 

I looked at several websites for inspiration and highlighted the pros and cons of each website and put toghether a moodboard

Selected Stylescape
Sketching
After studying the data and references i went on to design the basic wireframe for the website to have a clear overview of the page structures, flow, layout & orientation of the overall composition of the webpage.
