Challenge
Zambia, like many developing economies, faced challenges in terms of financial accessibility, especially in remote areas. The lack of convenient and affordable financial services prompted the need for an innovative solution. Broadpay set out to address these challenges by creating a mobile money app that caters to the diverse financial needs of the Zambian population.

Design Objectives
User-Friendly Interface : Develop an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface to ensure accessibility for users of all demographics.
Seamless Transactions : Prioritize a smooth and secure transaction process for sending and receiving money.
Inclusive Features : Incorporate features such as bill payments, account funding options, and instant customer support to create a comprehensive financial ecosystem.
Localized Experience : Tailor the app to the Zambian market, considering local preferences and needs.
Affordability: Prioritize fee-free transactions to make financial services accessible to all, irrespective of income level.

My Role & Approach

Conducted extensive market research to understand the financial landscape in Zambia, user preferences, and the challenges faced by the target audience.

Created detailed user personas to represent the diverse demographic segments in Zambia, ensuring that the app meets the needs of a wide range of users.

Developed wireframes and prototypes, incorporating feedback from potential users to refine the app's design and functionality.

Adopted a clean and vibrant visual design that reflects the cultural context of Zambia. The color palette and graphics were chosen to resonate with the target audience.

User flow
These wireframes offer a foundational structure illustrating the placement of the app description and associated elements on the screen.
Onboarding
Streamlined and user-friendly onboarding process to introduce users to the app's functionalities. It's as if the app is extending a friendly hand, guiding you through its features effortlessly.
Sign up
No hoops to jump through; just a quick and secure registration, and you have an account. The ease of the sign-up ensures that accessing the app is a breeze, allowing you to dive into the exciting world of hassle-free financial management.
Send Money
Seamless and fee-free money transfers right in the palm of your hands. Sending money to friends and family across Zambia becomes as simple as a few taps on your screen. No hidden fees, no complications
Bill Payments
Instead of navigating through multiple platforms, Lenco Money centralises the experience. In one place, you can effortlessly take care of your financial responsibilities, all without incurring any extra fees.
Add money
