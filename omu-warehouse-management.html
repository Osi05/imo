Omu, a prominent logistics and distribution company, faced operational challenges in its warehouse due to the absence of a suitable industry scanner app. Manual processes were leading to errors, delays, and inefficiencies in inventory management and order fulfilment. In response to this critical gap, we embarked on the design and development of the Omu Warehouse Management App.
Goal
The primary goal was to create a bespoke solution that would seamlessly integrate with the warehouse environment, offering advanced scanning capabilities and efficient management features. The aim was to enhance operational efficiency, reduce errors, and provide real-time visibility into inventory levels.
Role and Approach
As the design team, our role was to conceptualize and implement a user-centric solution that addressed the specific needs of the logistics and distribution industry. Our approach involved:

User Research:

Interviews and observations identified the need for a specialized industry scanner app.

Collaborative Ideation:

Collaborated with stakeholders to define important features and improve item management efficiency.

User flow and Iteration:

User flows were refined through iterative testing and user feedback.

User Training and Support:

Provided training materials and assistance during the app rollout, addressing concerns for a smooth transition and promoting user adoption.

These wireframes offer a foundational structure illustrating the placement of the app description and associated elements on the screen.
