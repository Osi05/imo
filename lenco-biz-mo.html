Background
Lenco launched in 2021 as a digital only business bank supporting business of all sizes and enabling business owners create and manage a business current account without visiting a physical outlet and overcoming the hassles of opening an account with the vision of becoming the go to business bank for all black owned businesses
I joined the team after the MVP was released and the initial framework used to build the banking system had to be changed so we decided to redesign the onboarding flow and the banking dashboard
Goal
The goal of this particular project was to identify any usability issues with the beta release and redesign the Lenco banking platform to improve the user experience of new and existing Lenco users, deliver customer satisfaction, faster onboarding for new users and determine the critical features to prioritise for the next iteration of the product.

My Role & Approach
As the lead designer i was responsible for optimising the onboarding flow, redesiging the banking dashboard and developing UX for new features. First i had to understand the user goals and needs, taking note of the user journey, uncovering pain points with the existing MVP and areas that could be improved to have the greatest impact on helping business owners open an account seamlessly and manage their finances better.
I looked at

Support issues : After working closely with the business support unit listening to their insights so far and feedback from customers i had a better understanding of the business and operations of Lenco  

In-product feedback to learn about the experience of current users and what’s top of their mind

 

Phone calls with exisiting users to learn more about their specific use cases and to validate open questions we had form business support and feedback

Based on the challenges identified, I worked towards addressing these pains by coming up with potential solutions:

Optimised the onboarding process for new users by Reducing the number of steps to minimise time to completion, surfacing mandatory fields and enabling the Next button to show error validation, establishing clearer visual form hierarchy by grouping related fields

 

Created a new design system to guide the team in the design of future products and help new team members catch on faster

Design system
To maintain consistency and ensure efficient design to dev handover, I developed a design system based on reusable components and their states, such as cards, list items, and controls. This has helped to maintain consistency in the look and feel across different parts of the platform, and also helped the Engineering and Product teams to understand how and why we choose to implement certain components over others.
